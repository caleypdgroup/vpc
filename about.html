Need an Inspection?

Termites are of particular importance when buying or selling a home since a termite inspection/infestation report is normally a condition of sale. While termites are probably the best known, there are also other wood destroying organisms such as Powder Post Beetles, Old House Borers and wood decaying fungi that can infest a structure that needs to be inspected for their activity or previous infestation.
